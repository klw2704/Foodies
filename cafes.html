Flat White

Serving some of Durham's best coffee and highly acclaimed food. Make sure that get there early on the weekends though, as the queue can often go out the door.

Leonard's Coffee House

One of Durham City's most popular eateries serving an exclusive coffee blend, homemade food and freshly baked sweet treats. A great brunch recommendation.

Cafédral

A small, family run business in Durham City with an eclectic taste for style and food. Full of quirky furniture and delicious food, this is a great little café to meet and catch up with your friends.

Café Sisters

Cafe Sisters is located in the oldest building in Silver Street, Durham. Serving a selection of teas, coffees and delicious food, this café has bare brick walls that gives it lots of character.

9 Altars

This cafe sits in the shadows of Durham City’s magnificent cathedral and is place to go if you're looking for a big slice of freshly baked cake.
